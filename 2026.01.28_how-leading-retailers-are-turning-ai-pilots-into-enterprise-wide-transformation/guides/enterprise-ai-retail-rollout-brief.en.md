# Enterprise AI rollout brief (Retail)

## Purpose
A 1-page executive briefing format for moving from AI pilots to enterprise-wide transformation, using the three-step framework described in the source post.

## Step 1: Lay the foundation (alignment + governance)
- Align on target business outcomes.
- Define governance for allowed use cases, data access/privacy, evaluation, and approvals.

## Step 2: Launch carefully selected pilots
- Pick a specific operational problem.
- Prove value within weeks.
- Define success metrics and a scale path.

## Step 3: Scale what works
- Standardize winning patterns and reuse governance.
- Expand to adjacent workflows.
- Build organizational capability (training + internal support).

## Notes from examples in the post
- Shopify: natural language → ShopifyQL (Sidekick).
- L’Oréal: 15+ specialized agents orchestrated for insights/visualizations.
- Lotte Homeshopping: 24/7 supplier support assistant.

## What is this post?
This post summarizes a retail-focused enterprise AI guide and highlights how leading retailers move from small AI pilots to organization-wide transformation.

## When is it useful?
Use it if you are planning an enterprise AI program and need a practical, executive-friendly framework for selecting pilots, setting governance, and scaling what works.

## Key points
- Retail AI efforts often stall after early pilots; the guide focuses on what differentiates organizations that scale.
- The post highlights examples: Shopify’s Sidekick translating natural-language questions into ShopifyQL, L’Oréal’s multi-agent system orchestrating 15+ specialized agents, and Lotte Homeshopping’s supplier support assistant.
- The guide’s “three steps” framing: establish alignment and governance, launch carefully selected lower-risk pilots, and scale successful use cases while building capability.

## Bundled resources
- One Agent Skill that turns the post’s “three steps” into a reusable rollout checklist and operating model for enterprise AI programs.
- One Guide (4 languages) that presents the same framework in an executive briefing format.

# Three-step rollout checklist (retail enterprise AI)

## Step 1: Foundation (alignment + governance)
- [ ] Target business outcomes are defined.
- [ ] Stakeholders are aligned on priorities and constraints.
- [ ] Governance exists for data access, privacy, evaluation, and approvals.

## Step 2: Pilots (lower-risk, prove value in weeks)
- [ ] Pilot solves a specific operational problem.
- [ ] Pilot can prove value within weeks.
- [ ] Success metrics are defined.
- [ ] Rollout plan exists (enablement, training, support).

## Step 3: Scale (standardize + build capability)
- [ ] Winning patterns are templatized.
- [ ] Governance and evaluation are reused consistently.
- [ ] Adjacent workflows are identified for expansion.
- [ ] Organizational capability plan exists (training + internal support).

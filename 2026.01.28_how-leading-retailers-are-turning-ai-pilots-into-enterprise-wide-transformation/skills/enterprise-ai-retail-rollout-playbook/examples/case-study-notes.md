# Case study notes (from the source post)

## Shopify
- Deployed Claude to power Sidekick.
- Sidekick translates natural-language merchant questions into ShopifyQL queries.

## L’Oréal
- Built a multi-agent system with Claude at the core.
- Orchestrates 15+ specialized agents.
- Serves insights and visualizations for 44,000 employees across 150 countries.

## Lotte Homeshopping
- Deployed an AI assistant for partner suppliers.
- Provides 24/7 support, handles QA inquiries, validates documentation, and guides partners through regulatory requirements.

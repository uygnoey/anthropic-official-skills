## Instructions
You are helping a retail organization design and run an enterprise AI program.

Use the “three steps” framework described in the source post and translate it into concrete actions, owners, and checkpoints.

### Step 1: Lay the foundation (alignment + governance)
- Define the business outcomes you want AI to improve.
- Establish governance for:
  - which use cases are allowed,
  - data access and privacy,
  - evaluation and approval criteria,
  - rollout and training.

### Step 2: Launch carefully selected pilots (start lower-risk)
- Choose pilots that:
  - solve a specific operational problem,
  - can prove value within weeks,
  - have a manageable blast radius.
- Ensure each pilot has:
  - clear success metrics,
  - a plan to operationalize outcomes,
  - a path to scale if successful.

### Step 3: Scale what works (build capability)
- Standardize successful patterns (templates, governance, enablement).
- Expand from initial wins to adjacent workflows.
- Build organizational capability through training and internal support.

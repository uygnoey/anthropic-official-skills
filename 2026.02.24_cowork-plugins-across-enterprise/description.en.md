## What is this post?
This post announces updates to Cowork and plugins aimed at enterprise teams, including admin-controlled private marketplaces and broader connector/plugin availability.

## When is it useful?
It’s useful when you want to distribute consistent, role-specific Claude workflows across an organization with centralized controls (marketplaces, connector provisioning, and governance).

## Key points
- Admins can create organization-specific plugin marketplaces and manage distribution.
- Plugins are portable, file-based bundles that can include skills and connectors.
- Claude can work across apps like Excel and PowerPoint, carrying context between tools.

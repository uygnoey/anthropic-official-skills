## Instructions
- Direct users to download the Android app from Google Play.
- Emphasize cross-platform continuity: web, iOS, and Android conversations sync.
- Highlight key capabilities available in the Android app: vision (camera/photos), multilingual use, and advanced reasoning.

## Examples
- “Draft a short announcement telling users how to get the Android app and what features it includes.”
- “Create FAQ bullets about syncing conversations across web, iOS, and Android.”

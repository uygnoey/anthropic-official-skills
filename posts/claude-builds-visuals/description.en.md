# Claude builds interactive visuals right in your conversation

## What is this post?
This post announces an in-chat capability where Claude can create temporary, interactive charts, diagrams, and visualizations inline to support understanding during a conversation.

## When is it useful?
Use it when you want a concept or dataset explained visually (for example, asking for a diagram or a time-varying visualization) and then iterating on the visual as the conversation continues.

## Key points
- These visuals are different from artifacts: they are inline and temporary, changing or disappearing as the conversation evolves.
- The feature is on by default; Claude may create a visual proactively, or you can ask directly.
- You can request modifications after a visual appears (adjustments, deeper exploration).

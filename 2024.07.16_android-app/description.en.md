## What is this post?
This post announces the release of the Claude Android app.

## When is it useful?
Use it if you want to access Claude on Android and continue conversations across web, iOS, and Android.

## Key points
- Claude is available on Android via the Google Play app.
- Conversations sync across web, iOS, and Android.
- The Android app supports vision (camera/photos), multilingual use, and advanced reasoning.

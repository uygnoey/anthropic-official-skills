## What is this post?
This post announces new consumer-facing app connectors available in Claude, expanding beyond work tools to everyday apps.

## When is it useful?
It’s useful when you want Claude to pull relevant information from the apps you already use (e.g., travel planning, shopping, listening, taxes) within your conversations.

## Key points
- The connector directory has expanded significantly (over 200 connectors since July 2025).
- New connectors include AllTrails, Audible, Booking.com, Instacart, TripAdvisor, Intuit TurboTax, and others.
- Connectors can appear dynamically in conversations, and you control what you choose to view and share.

## What is this post?
Anthropic summarizes Microsoft’s announcement that **Claude models are available in Microsoft 365 Copilot**, starting with the Researcher agent and Copilot Studio.

## When is it useful?
- When enterprise users want to access Claude through Microsoft’s productivity environment.
- When organizations need model choice in Microsoft 365 Copilot experiences (Researcher or Copilot Studio).

## Key points
- Microsoft 365 Copilot customers can choose **Claude Sonnet 4** and **Claude Opus 4.1** as model options (via Microsoft’s Frontier Program at rollout).
- Claude Opus 4.1 can power Microsoft’s **Researcher agent** for multistep research across web, third-party data, and work content.
- Claude models are also available in **Copilot Studio** as options for building and managing enterprise-grade agents.
- Anthropic notes the models are hosted outside Microsoft-managed environments and subject to Anthropic Terms and Conditions.

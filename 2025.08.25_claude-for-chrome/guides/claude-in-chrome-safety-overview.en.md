## Overview
This guide summarizes safe usage and core defenses described for Claude in Chrome.

## Safe usage checklist (from the post)
- Use site-level permissions to limit access, and review/adjust permissions in Settings.
- Expect confirmations before high-risk actions like publishing, purchasing, or sharing personal data.
- Start on trusted sites and be mindful of what information is visible.
- Avoid using the extension on sites involving financial, legal, medical, or other sensitive information.

## Defenses highlighted
- Blocklists for high-risk website categories.
- Classifiers for suspicious instruction patterns and unusual data access requests.
- Mitigations for browser-specific attacks (e.g., hidden form fields in the DOM).

## What is this post?
Anthropic announces **Claude for Enterprise**, an enterprise plan focused on secure, organization-wide collaboration with Claude, including an expanded 500K context window, higher usage capacity, and enterprise security/admin controls.

## When is it useful?
- When an organization needs centralized controls (SSO, role-based permissions, auditability) for deploying Claude.
- When teams need to work with large internal corpora (long documents, many transcripts, or sizeable codebases) in one place.

## Key points
- Enterprise plan includes a **500K context window**, increased capacity, and enterprise admin features.
- Includes security features like **SSO/domain capture**, **role-based access**, and plans for **audit logs** and **SCIM**.
- Mentions a **native GitHub integration** (beta at announcement) to sync repositories with Claude.
- Anthropic states it does not train Claude on customer conversations and content.

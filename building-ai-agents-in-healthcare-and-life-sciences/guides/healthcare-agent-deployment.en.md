# Deploying AI agents in healthcare and life sciences (guide)

This guide condenses the post into a deployment-focused decision flow.

## 1) Interoperability decisions
1. Choose connectivity (direct integration, custom connectors such as APIs/MCP, or middleware).
2. Standardize data ingestion and conversions.
3. Define synchronization (real-time vs batch) based on clinical urgency.

## 2) Safety and compliance controls
- Establish governance, audit trails, and operational monitoring.
- Validate clinical impact claims with evidence.

## 3) Human oversight model
- Define escalation and override paths.
- Default to safe behavior.

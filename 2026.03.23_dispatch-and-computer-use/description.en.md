## What is this post?
This post announces that Claude can use your computer (apps, browser, and developer tools) to complete tasks, and introduces Dispatch for assigning those tasks from your phone.

## When is it useful?
Use it when you want Claude to carry out multi-step work in your existing apps and workflows—especially when connectors aren’t available and UI interaction is needed.

## Key points
- Claude Cowork and Claude Code can point, click, and navigate to complete work in your apps.
- Claude prioritizes precise tools like connectors first, and falls back to controlling the screen, mouse, and keyboard when needed.
- It asks for explicit permission before taking actions, and includes safeguards (e.g., scanning for prompt injection and restricting certain apps by default).
- Dispatch lets you assign tasks from your phone and continue the same conversation on desktop.

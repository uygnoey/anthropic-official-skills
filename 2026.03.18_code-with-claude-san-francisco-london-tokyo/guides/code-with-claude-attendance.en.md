## What this guide covers
A quick plan for attending Code with Claude in-person or watching remotely.

## Steps
- Choose a city/date (San Francisco: May 6, 2026; London: May 19, 2026; Tokyo: June 10, 2026).
- If attending in-person: apply for a seat (space is limited; selection is by random drawing).
- Register for the livestream if you plan to watch remotely.
- Watch for the agenda preview (the post says to check back in early April).
- Watch for applicant notifications (the post says early April).

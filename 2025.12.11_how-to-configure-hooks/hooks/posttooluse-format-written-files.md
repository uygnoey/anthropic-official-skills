# PostToolUse: auto-format files after Write/Edit

Runs `prettier` after `Write` or `Edit` completes, using the `$CLAUDE_TOOL_INPUT_FILE_PATH` environment variable.

## Install

1. Copy `posttooluse-format-written-files.json` into your hooks configuration.
2. Ensure `prettier` is available on your PATH.

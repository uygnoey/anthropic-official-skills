## What is this post?
This post introduces a downloadable guide on building Claude skills, covering planning, structure, testing/iteration, and distribution.

## When is it useful?
Use it when you want Claude to apply your workflows consistently by packaging instructions, scripts, and references into a reusable skill.

## Key points
- Skills are packaged folders that teach Claude a repeatable workflow.
- Good skills balance progressive disclosure: keep SKILL.md concise and link to bundled references/scripts.
- The guide emphasizes planning concrete use cases, testing for triggering/false triggers, and iterating from real failures.

## Bundled resources
- An Agent Skill summarizing the guide’s recommended SKILL.md outline and a testing checklist.

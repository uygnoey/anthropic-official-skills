## What is this post?
This post describes Cowork updates for finance teams, including new finance plugins, new MCP connectors (FactSet and MSCI), and workflows that span Excel and PowerPoint.

## When is it useful?
Use these ideas when finance deliverables require moving from data to models to slides, and you want Claude to keep context across tools while following finance-specific workflow patterns.

## Key points
- Cross-app workflows let Claude move between Excel and PowerPoint without losing context.
- Five finance plugins are introduced: financial analysis, investment banking, equity research, private equity, and wealth management.
- Partner integrations are highlighted (FactSet, MSCI, LSEG, and S&P Global).

## What is this post?
This post announces updates to the Claude Team plan, including new pricing and an annual discount for premium seats.

## When is it useful?
It’s useful if you’re evaluating Claude Team for a growing organization and want to understand the updated seat pricing and what the plan includes.

## Key points
- Standard seats: $20/month with annual billing (or $25/month billed monthly).
- Premium seats: $100/month with annual billing (or $125/month billed monthly).
- Team includes a shared workspace, admin controls, billing management, privacy defaults, connectors for internal knowledge, and Claude Code with every seat.

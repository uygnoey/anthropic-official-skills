## What is this post?
This post introduces Cowork plugins, which bundle file-based components (skills, connectors, slash commands, and sub-agents) to customize how Cowork gets work done.

## When is it useful?
Useful when you want consistent, role-specific workflows for teams (e.g., sales, legal, finance) and want to package guidance and tools into a shareable plugin.

## Key points
- Plugins can bundle skills, connectors, slash commands, and sub-agents into a reusable specialist package.
- Plugins are file-based, making them easy to build, edit, and share.
- Anthropic released an open-source collection of 11 plugins spanning common business functions.
- Plugins are currently saved locally to your machine, with improved org-wide management planned.

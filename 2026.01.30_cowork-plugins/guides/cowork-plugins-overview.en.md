## What is this post?
Cowork plugins bundle file-based components (skills, connectors, slash commands, and sub-agents) so you can customize how Cowork performs work for a role, team, or company.

## When is it useful?
Use plugins when you want consistent outcomes across a team and you want to package your preferred workflows, tooling, and context in a form that’s easy to build, edit, and share.

## Key points
- Plugins can bundle skills, connectors, slash commands, and sub-agents into one reusable package.
- Because components are file-based, plugins are easy to maintain and distribute.
- The post highlights an open-source collection of 11 plugins across common functions (productivity, enterprise search, sales, finance, data, legal, marketing, customer support, product management, biology research, and plugin creation/customization).
- Plugins are currently stored locally on your machine.

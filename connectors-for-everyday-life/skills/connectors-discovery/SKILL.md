## Instructions
Use this skill when you want to:
- Identify which connector(s) may help answer a question.
- Ask Claude to incorporate information from a connected app in a controlled way.
- Keep track of what you’ve shared (and avoid sharing unnecessary data).

Guidelines:
1. Ask a clear, outcome-focused question (what you want to decide or produce).
2. If multiple connectors could help, ask Claude to suggest which connector to use and why.
3. Confirm what information will be pulled in before acting on it.
4. Prefer minimal disclosure: share only what’s needed to complete the task.

## Examples
- “Which connector should I use to plan a weekend hiking trip, and what info would you need from me before you pull anything in?”
- “Use the TripAdvisor connector to summarize the top 3 neighborhoods to stay in for a first-time visit, and tell me what sources you used.”
- “I want to compare prices across a few grocery lists. Suggest how to use the Instacart connector, and ask me for confirmation before you access my account data.”

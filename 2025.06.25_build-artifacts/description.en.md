## What is this post?
This product post introduces **Artifacts** in the Claude app: a dedicated space to build, browse, customize, organize, and share interactive creations, including the ability to embed AI capabilities into artifacts (beta).

## When is it useful?
Use Artifacts when you want to turn an idea into a shareable app-like experience (tools, games, learning aids) through conversation, without writing code.

## Key points
- Artifacts are a way to turn conversations into interactive creations.
- Claude adds an artifacts space to browse curated examples, customize existing artifacts, build from scratch, and organize your work.
- Updates mentioned: availability on mobile apps, Team/Enterprise plans, and later support for MCP and persistent storage.

## Bundled resources
- The post does not provide reusable Claude Code configs (skills/hooks/output styles); it describes product capabilities and how to access them in the Claude app.

# Claude on Google Cloud’s Vertex AI: FedRAMP High and IL2 Authorized

## What is this post?
Announces that Claude models are authorized for FedRAMP High and DoD Impact Level 2 (IL2) workloads on Google Cloud Vertex AI, enabling federal and defense organizations to use Claude while meeting strict compliance requirements.

## When is it useful?
Useful when you need to evaluate or communicate whether Claude can be used in regulated U.S. government environments on Google Cloud, or when planning an internal rollout on Vertex AI under FedRAMP High / IL2 controls.

## Key points
- Claude models are available via Google Cloud Vertex AI for environments authorized up to FedRAMP High and IL2.
- Assured Workloads is called out as the mechanism for government-focused compliance controls on Google Cloud.
- The post emphasizes managed/serverless access via APIs to avoid infrastructure management while meeting security needs.
- Positions FedRAMP High and IL2 availability as progress toward future higher-impact authorizations (e.g., IL5).

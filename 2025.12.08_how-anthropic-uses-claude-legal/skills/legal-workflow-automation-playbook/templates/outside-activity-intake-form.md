# Outside business activity intake form (template)

Fields mentioned in the post:
- Department
- Manager
- Description of the proposed activity

Optional additions (keep aligned to your COI policy):
- Time commitment
- Compensation (if any)
- Relationship to existing vendors/partners/customers
- Potential overlap with company work

## Output expectations
- Ask follow-up questions if the description is insufficient.
- Provide a recommendation for legal review, but leave final approval to a lawyer.

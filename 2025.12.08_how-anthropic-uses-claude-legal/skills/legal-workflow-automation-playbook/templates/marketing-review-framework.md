# Marketing content review framework (template)

Use this template to encode your team’s “historical guidance and review framework” for marketing content review.

## Scope
- What content is covered (blog posts, emails, landing pages, ads, social posts)
- What is out of scope and should be escalated

## Issue categories to check
### Publicity rights / brand use
- Do we have rights to use any referenced names, logos, likenesses, or testimonials?
- Are there required attributions or trademark symbols?

### Claims and substantiation
- Are claims overstated?
- Are claims supported by the referenced evidence?
- If a statistic is used: is it accurate, current, and properly sourced?

### Regulatory / compliance considerations
- Any jurisdiction-specific constraints (if applicable)
- Any mandatory disclosures

## Risk scoring
- Low: minor cleanup; safe to proceed with suggested edits.
- Medium: needs human review; proceed only after changes.
- High: stop and escalate; do not publish until resolved.

## Output format
- Summary
- Flagged issues with: category, risk level, rationale, suggested fix
- Questions to ask the requester (if additional info is required)
